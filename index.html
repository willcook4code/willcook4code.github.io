Pointer App

Building the Pointer App was exciting for many reasons. Our class of 10 front end programmers used the Scrum method, along with a Trello board to build out our user stories and break out the project into steps. The site was a prototype for a real-life client who was interested in constructing a tool for children to follow along with a story as it is read to them. The word being read is underlined to help with audio/visual association. It was a great opportunity to practice version control on github. We used React, backbone, jQuery, Postgres/SQL, Express, Node.js, and Sass.
